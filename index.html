About Me

I am an Associate Professor at NUST College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering, specializing in mechatronics, electromechanical systems, and computational modeling of smart materials. My research includes piezoelectric materials, tactile force sensors, and automation systems. I have published in high-impact journals and serve as Editor in numerous Impact Factor Journals.
